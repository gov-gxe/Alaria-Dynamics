Portal Access & Gating

Aaria Dynamics restricts sensitive technical materials to authorized partners and government customers. Access tiers:

Tier 0 — Public

General marketing materials, public brochures, press releases.

Tier 1 — Registered

Commercial partner access after account verification. Requires business email, company details, and approved terms.

Tier 2 — Government / Authorized Buyers

Requires organizational verification, procurement ID, and NDAs. Access to gated technical documents and datasheets.

Tier 3 — Classified / Secure

Internal or cleared personnel only. Requires SSO with SAML/OIDC + client certificates + 2FA and network restrictions. Not exposed on this public demo.

Registration workflow

  1. Register with business email and organization details.
  2. Automated vetting (domain checks) then manual review for Tier 2.
  3. Sign NDA and receive account provisioning.
  4. For Tier 3, complete additional security onboarding (SSO, certificates, IP allowlist).

Production systems must implement audit logs for downloads, short token lifetimes, and regular compliance checks (ISO/NIST).
